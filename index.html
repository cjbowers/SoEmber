A Unique Ember Experience

So Ember Conf is focused on advancing the technology and community around Ember. The main event stars 7 showcase speakers and 1 spectacular keynote. Connect during the delightful pre-conference festivities or relax together during the after-conference event.

Lodging

Lodging will be provided at a discounted rate by the conference venue, Catamaran Resort & Spa, with a 15% discount for attendees based on room availability. Rates fluctuate with occupancy and will tend to increase as your event date approaches. Holidays and peak times of the year may require a multiple night minimum stay.

To make room reservations, please call the Reservations Center at 1-800-422-8386 and mention "DockYard - Annual Conference".

Parking at the Catamaran Resort & Spa will be $10 a day for valet or $23 overnight for guests, $28 for overnight guests valetted.

Other accomodations are available nearby: Blue Sea Beach Hotel, Crystal Pier Hotel & Cottages & Tower 23
